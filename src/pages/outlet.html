Description

A shopping center located in Dubai, the outlet village offers iconic fashion brands like Michael Kors and Dolce & Gabbana. The project goal is to create an online shopping option for customers who are unable to go to the mall because of the pandemic. Other features include login functionality, rewards, and delivery options.


I conceptualized the first sketches for this project using Adobe XD and their user flow. Additionally, I introduced some gamification features such as reward and promotion stages.
