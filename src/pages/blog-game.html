How to achieve out before dessert trophy

Resident Evil 7 (RE7) is a first-person survival horror shooting game that takes place somewhere in the resident evil saga. Many trophies are available in RE7, such as playing the game without using green herbs, guns, or other items. Out before dessert is one of the most difficult trophies to win. The game requires you to locate the Happy Birthday videotape and find an old television to play it on before you can solve the puzzle within five minutes.


You need to follow each topic in chronological order for you to achieve the out before dessert trophy. First things first, find the happy birthday videotape, which is the next topic below.


Locating the Happy Birthday videotape

    To start the out before dessert trophy you need to grab the happy birthday videotape located in the kid’s room attic. To get to the room, you need to do the following steps.


  • 1.

    Get the Crow key outside the Old House in the Outhouse by the Pump Station.

  • 2.

    Kill the mutated Margarite boss.

  • 3.

    Go back to Main house B1 towards Dissection Room.

  • 4.

    Take the Snake key on the police corpse.

  • 5.

    Go to the top floor of the house which is the Kid’s room attic, and use the snake key to open it.

  • 6.

    Check the shelf beside the bed to see the happy birthday VHS.

Finding a TV to play the happy birthday puzzle

The nearest and easiest way to find a TV is by going into the yard. Take note that there’s a mold and a monster along the way. Ready your gun and shoot all of them to get into the trailer truck. Once inside the trailer truck, go to the farthest right of the truck to see the couch. The TV and a VHS player are sitting beside each other. Run the happy birthday VHS to play the puzzle.


Solving the happy birthday puzzle

The following are the steps you need to take when solving the puzzle. Take note that you might do a re-run if you’re not able to reach the specific time frame. For doing a re-run, stop the video on the pause menu and put it back on the VHS.


  • 1.

    Get the candle that is on the arm of the clown in front of you.

  • 2.

    Go to the next room where you can see the birthday cake.

  • 3.

    In this room, there’s a barrel that has a winder and a safety box with a lock.

  • 4.

    Take the winder and open the safety box with the below picture.

  • 5.

    Take the doll inside the safety box and exit the room.

  • 6.

    Go back to the previous room where the stove is and light up the doll.

  • 7.

    Burning the doll will provide you with a finger toy.

  • 8.

    Light up the candle again and run to the door with the rope on it.

  • 9.

    Burn the rope that is holding the door for you to be able to enter.

  • 10.

    Grab the yellow balloon beside the rack.

  • 11.

    Go back to the room where the stove is, to find an open pipe.

  • 12.

    Put the yellow balloon on the pipe to make it pop.

  • 13.

    Once the balloon explodes, you will get the quill pen.

  • 14.

    Go back to the clown to put the finger, quill pen, and the winder.

  • 15.

    Now you will have the password to enter the last locked door.

  • 16.

    Now you will have the password to enter the last locked door.

  • 17.

    Go back to the birthday cake room and put the valve on the pipe.

  • 18.

    Now the room is dry you can light up your candle again.

  • 19.

    Put the candle on the cake to finish the puzzle.
