Born and raised in the Philippines, a User Experience (UX) designer who loves to travel, soak up new experiences, and try various things. With a background in Computer Science, my passion is developing web applications that solve problems for people and provide businesses with opportunities to grow.


In addition to my formal education, I have taken online courses, participated in workshops, read books, and applied UX principles to several projects. I enjoy putting complex problems into simple, beautiful interface designs. Additionally, I'm fascinated by the logic and structure of coding, and I strive to code in an elegant and efficient manner, whether it's HTML, CSS, or Javascript.

In 2022, I began studying software development at the Manitoba Institute of Trades and Technology (MITT). MITT provides a fast-phased learning opportunity that helps you become a well-versed software developer.
